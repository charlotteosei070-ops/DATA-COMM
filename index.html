How a Layman Understands the Telegraph

1

The Button (Key)

Think of it like a doorbell button. When you press it, electricity flows.

2

The Wire

Just like a garden hose carries water, the wire carries electricity from point A to point B.

3

The Sounder

At the other end, a metal bar clicks when electricity arrives. Short click = dot, long click = dash.
